Detailed Directions from the South (MA and beyond)
Take 95 North into New Hampshire towards Portsmouth.
Make sure you stay to the right as you approach Portsmouth.
Take exit 5 toward the Portsmouth Traffic Circle
Get in the left hand lane as you approach the traffic circle.
Carefully enter the traffic circle and go halfway around (180 degrees).
Continue on Route 1 for about 3/4 of a mile.
Take the Maplewood Ave exit to the right. Be careful it is not very well marked.
At the end of the exit, bear right onto Maplewood Avenue..
In 100 yards, take a sharp right onto Dennett Street.
56 Dennett is the 5th house on the left.
